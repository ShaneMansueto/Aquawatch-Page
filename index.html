Features

Everything you need to
protect your water supply

Five powerful tools working together so your community always has safe, verified water information at their fingertips.

Strip-to-Scan Testing

Use your phone camera to scan DIY water testing strips. Our AI instantly reads and logs contamination levels, turning your device into a portable water lab.

Real-Time Safety Map

See a live heatmap of water safety across your neighborhood. Color-coded zones and community pins show outages, alerts, and safe sources nearby.

Outage & Pressure Alerts

Get instant push notifications when water service drops in your area. Know before you turn the tap — and report outages to alert your neighbors in seconds.

Direct Authority Reports

Submit verified contamination data directly to local water boards and government agencies — closing the loop between citizens and officials who can act.

How It Works

Scan. Report. Protect.

Three simple steps — and your entire community benefits from real-time water intelligence.

1

Pin Your Location & Source

Open AquaWatch and drop a pin on any water source — tap, spring, fountain, or well. Add a photo and label the source type.

2

Test & Scan Your Strip

Run a DIY test strip and hold it to your camera. Our AI reads pH, minerals, bacteria risk, and turbidity — results in under 10 seconds.

3

Submit & Earn

Hit submit. Your data joins the live map instantly, alerts nearby users, and is sent to local authorities if thresholds are breached. Earn Guardian points for every verified report.

4

Track Impact & Stay Updated

Watch your reports get resolved. Receive follow-up notifications when officials address issues you flagged — and see your community's water safety score rise.
